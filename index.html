Crystal Collector!

Let's play crystal collector!!!

It's the hip new game from Evan Inc.

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


THere are four crystals below. By clicking on one crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your score


You will win the game by matching your total score to the random number given, you lose if your total score goes above the number given


The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time when the game starts, the value of each crystal will change.
